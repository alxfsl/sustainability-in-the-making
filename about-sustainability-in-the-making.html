About Sustainability in the Making 

Sustainability in the Making is a PhD project that explores how cultures of making can contribute to sustainability change.

In other words, my project aims to understand the role of craft programmes and initiatives as social enabler for generating and promoting cultures of sustainability

In this page you can find updates on the my project, from small essays, community activities, and information on how to get involved.
