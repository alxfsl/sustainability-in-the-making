Alessandra Fasoli

I'm a design researcher and a PhD candidate at Kingston School of Art, London. Sustainability in the Making is my craft-based PhD where I am trying to grasp how cultures of sustainability are generated through collective craft, through my craft: drawing and web design. I am the designer and researcher behind this website, which is meant a part of the PhD research process. 

I also work as a lecturer and a freelance designer, occasionally taking illustration commissions. 

My main areas of research are design anthropology, sustainability, critical theory, and environmental sociology.

I draw. A lot
