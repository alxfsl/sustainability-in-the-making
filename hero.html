Sapienza University of Rome

In June 2021, I announced my research at the Cumulus Conference 2021 "Design Culture(s)", hosted by Sapienza University of Rome. At the time of the call for papers (2019, the conference originally was planned in 2020, but it was postponed due to COVID-19 pandemic), my research was at a very early stages. The very first poster I submitted was still very vague in its approach, but as in 2020 I definitely move to the UK, I decided to focus the research on the observation of local maker communities. Because of health restrictions in place in 2021, I attended the conference online, where I presented my early plans for my research in the dedicated poster section.¬†
