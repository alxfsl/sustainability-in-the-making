Sustainability in the Making (former title: Craft in Makerspaces: The Potential for Social Change for Sustainability) is a practice-based doctoral training partnership sponsored by AHRC Techne.

Based at Kingston School of Art and done in partnership with the Crafts Council, my research started as an investigation of the cultural role of makerspaces. However, following considerations emerging from social and environmental changes over the last few years, I decided to take a broader social and cultural perspective on making which goes a bit beyond makerspaces.
