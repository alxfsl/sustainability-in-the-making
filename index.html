Sustainability in the Making

Craft Programmes for Eco-Social Change

A craft-based PhD that explores how cultures of making can contribute to sustainability change.

Sustainability in the Making ¬†(formerly: Craft in Makerspaces. The Potential for Social Change for Sustainability) is a practice-based doctoral training partnership sponsored by AHRC Techne based at Kingston School of Art in partnership with the Crafts Council.


It is also an EthnoGRAPHIC journey through craft, drawings, and makers' stories.
